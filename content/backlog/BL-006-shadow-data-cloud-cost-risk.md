## Angle
Bridge FinOps and security with a practical assessment framework. CTOs who think they've already cleaned house haven't looked at the data sprawl.

## Why this fills a gap
Existing FinOps drafts focus on compute and ADX costs. No draft addresses storage data sprawl as both a cost and security problem.
