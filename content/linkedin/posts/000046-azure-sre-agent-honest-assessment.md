I demoed something to the FinOps team at Microsoft. They went a different direction.

So I open-sourced it.

16,000 lines of PowerShell turned into 200 lines of YAML. The idea was simple: the team that sees the bill and the team that manages the resources should be looking at the same signals at the same time.

I wrote about what I built, why it matters, and the gap most organizations have between seeing a cost problem and actually fixing it.

Who acts on the FinOps recommendations at your company? If the answer is "a ticket queue", you might want to read this.
