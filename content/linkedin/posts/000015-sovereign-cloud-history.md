I had a strong belief a few years ago: sovereign cloud was the inevitable end state for regulated enterprises.

Then I kept watching what happened in production. Teams still needed global features, global talent pools, and global interoperability. The sovereignty controls were added. The separate infrastructure model kept collapsing under cost and complexity.

I wrote this post to separate what was a legitimate 2013 security reaction from what is still economically defensible in 2026.

Where is your organization on this journey right now: still evaluating sovereign-only models, or standardizing on regional controls over global cloud platforms?
