Every AI agent framework invents its own way to call tools. Its own auth. Its own error handling.

Then someone asks: "Can we connect this agent to our ticketing system, our database, and our monitoring platform?"

That is where it falls apart.

Anthropic proposed a standard protocol to fix this. The ecosystem is moving fast. The enterprise friction points are moving faster.

I wrote about what MCP actually solves, where it breaks, and the one question every enterprise team should ask before adopting it.

Is your team building on MCP yet, or waiting to see who blinks first?
