I had 100 blog posts stuck in draft on my old WordPress blog. Real drafts, not notes. Titles, intros, half-written arguments.

Moved to a new platform on March 1st. Already have 50 drafts here. It is not the platform. It is clearly me.

Solo bloggers have no editing team. No fact-checkers. No production schedule. You are every role at once, and the role that always gets skipped is the one that finishes the draft.

So I built an agentic AI workflow. Over 20 specialized agents organized into a pipeline: signals scout, research analyst, outline architect, draft writer, fact-risk reviewer, security reviewer, contrarian reviewer, voice editor, revision loop, SEO editor, and a publisher.

Every post runs through the same gates. If it fails any gate, it does not advance.

That pipeline has already failed me once. I fixed the loophole. I will find more.

Does it work? I am not sure yet. This blog is the experiment. I will find out by publishing consistently for the next year.
