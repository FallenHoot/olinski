# Audience Signals: Cloud Resource Hoarding Under Capacity Pressure

## Audience Problem
Enterprise platform and FinOps leaders are treating low utilization as waste when part of that utilization is intentionally held as insurance against constrained reacquisition. They need a decision framework that balances cost efficiency with reliability risk.

## Audience Alignment
- CTOs and VP Engineering responsible for reliability and cost outcomes
- FinOps leaders driving utilization and commitment strategy
- Cloud architects designing multi-region and AI capacity plans
- SRE leaders managing service-level risk during supply constraints

## Reader Intent
- Understand why hoarding appears in mature organizations
- Distinguish bad overprovisioning from risk-priced reserve behavior
- Adopt operating patterns that reduce both incident risk and stranded spend
