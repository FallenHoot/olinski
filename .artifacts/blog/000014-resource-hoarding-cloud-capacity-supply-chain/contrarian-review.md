# Contrarian Review: Cloud Resource Hoarding Under Capacity Pressure

## Strongest Counterarguments

1. Some hoarding is still poor governance, not rational hedging
2. Utilization targets can be corrected without redefining the supply model
3. Multi-layer supply-chain framing may feel too macro for application teams
4. Organizations with long-term commitments may not face the same reacquisition risk

## Required Qualifications

- Clarify that not all idle capacity is justified
- Distinguish intentional risk buffer from unmanaged overprovisioning
- Emphasize that workload class determines acceptable reserve levels
- Note that regulated and sovereign workloads may have distinct constraints

## Publish Recommendation

Publish-ready with qualification language preserved. The post is strongest when framed as a decision model for constrained periods, not a universal defense of excess capacity.
