# Framework Pack: Extending CCoE

## AI Governance Additions to CCoE
1. Model Registry (approved models, licensing, terms)
2. Cost Controls (token budgets, monthly caps)
3. Compliance Checks (privacy, bias, data retention)
4. Training (AI fundamentals for teams)

## Phased Rollout
- Month 1: Model registry only
- Month 2: Add cost controls to review gate
- Month 3: Add compliance checks
- Month 4: Run training workshop

## CCoE AI Lead Responsibilities (10-20% FTE)
- Maintain model registry
- Define AI compliance policies
- Lead training sessions
- Report AI governance metrics to CCoE steering
