# Contrarian Review

## Strongest critique

The framework assumes governance should precede autonomy, but many teams will ship agents first and govern later.

## Counter-arguments to include

- Early governance prevents bigger compliance and security problems downstream.
- Shadow mode (agent recommends, human executes) bridges the gap for teams that want early autonomy.
- Regulatory pressure will force governance anyway; better to build it proactively.

## Credibility risks

- Sounding too prescriptive when industry is still learning.
- Over-emphasizing compliance risk without acknowledging business agility benefits of agents.

## Improvements applied

- Framed governance as enabler, not blocker.
- Included shadow-mode approach for teams wanting early deployment.
- Balanced risk with opportunity in "what changed" section.
