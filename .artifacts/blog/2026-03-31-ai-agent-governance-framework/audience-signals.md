# AI Agent Governance: The Framework No One Has Written Yet

## Audience signals

- Enterprise architects building agent governance policies
- CTOs and platform leads deploying agentic AI in regulated industries
- Compliance and audit teams evaluating agent risk
- DevOps teams managing agent scope and permissions

## Audience pain points

- No existing industry standard for agent governance
- Compliance teams uncertain how to audit agent decisions
- Teams shipping agents without clear decision authority or escalation rules
- Fear of agent drift, scope creep, or uncontrolled autonomous action

## Demand signals

- Rapid escalation of agentic AI deployments across enterprise
- High concern in regulated industries (financial, healthcare, compliance) about audit and accountability
- Growing gap between deployment speed and governance maturity

## Desired outcome

Provide a practical, team-scale governance framework that is compliant, auditable, and specific enough to implement immediately.
