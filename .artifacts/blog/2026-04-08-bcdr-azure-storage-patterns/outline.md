# Outline: BCDR for Azure Storage

1. **Opening:** Storage BCDR is harder than compute BCDR (data loss risk is real)
2. **Redundancy Options:** GRS, RA-GRS, GZRS, manual replication
3. **Cost Comparison:** Replication cost by region pair and frequency
4. **RPO/RTO Matrix:** What you get with each option
5. **Practical Patterns:** Application-level failover without manual intervention
6. **Monitoring Setup:** Detecting failover trigger conditions
7. **Action Checklist:** Week 1 assessment of current BCDR readiness
