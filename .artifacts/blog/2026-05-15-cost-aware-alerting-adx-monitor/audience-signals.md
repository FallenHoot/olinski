# Audience Signals: Cost-Aware Alerting with ADX + Monitor
## Audience Problem
Teams set up alerts in Azure Monitor without considering the cost of false positives. Too many alerts = alert fatigue + ignored alerts. Too few = missed incidents. No clear framework for cost-aware alerting (alerts should pay for themselves through prevented incidents).
## Audience Alignment
- SRE engineers
- FinOps teams
- Platform teams managing observability
