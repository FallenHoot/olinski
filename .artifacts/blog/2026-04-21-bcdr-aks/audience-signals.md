# Audience Signals: BCDR for AKS

## Audience Problem
Platform teams building on AKS need multi-region BCDR strategies. Questions arise: Do we replicate the whole cluster? Just data? Just configs? Teams are often unclear on what "disaster" means for AKS (node failure vs cluster failure vs region failure) and how recovery maps to each.

## Key Signals
- AKS disaster recovery guidance (Azure Learn)
- GitOps adoption enabling cluster config portability
- Increased multi-region AKS deployments (conference trends)

## Audience Alignment
- Platform engineers designing AKS infrastructure
- DevOps teams owning cluster operations
- CTO offices evaluating multi-region strategies
