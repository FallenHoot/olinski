# PTU vs TPM Audience Signals

## Target audience

- Enterprise procurement and finance teams evaluating Azure AI
- Platform and infrastructure leads managing AI costs
- CIOs deciding on capacity model for company-wide AI tooling

## Audience pain points

- Unsure whether to commit to PTU or stay flexible on TPM
- Vendor messaging is contradictory ("PTU saves money" vs "TPM gives flexibility")
- Finance wants cost certainty; eng wants flexibility
- Fear of runaway costs on TPM or wasted commitment on PTU

## Demand signals

- Growing adoption of Azure AI Foundry across enterprises
- Pricing and SKU model changes frequently; decision urgency is high
- Significant budget decisions hinge on capacity model choice

## Desired outcome

Provide a data-driven decision framework so enterprise teams can pick the right model based on their actual usage patterns and risk appetite.
