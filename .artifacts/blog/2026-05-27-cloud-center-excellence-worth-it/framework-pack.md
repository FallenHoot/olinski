# Framework Pack: CCoE Evolution

## CCoE Lifecycle Stages
1. **Formation (Year 0-1):** Establish governance, training, tooling. High ROI.
2. **Growth (Year 1-2):** Teams implement practices. ROI still positive.
3. **Maturity (Year 2-3):** Teams are cloud-savvy. CCoE role shifts.
4. **Evolution (Year 3+):** CCoE transforms into platform team or downsizes.

## Governance ROI Calculation
- CCoE cost: annual budget (people, tools, training)
- Benefits: cost avoidance, faster adoption, compliance maintained
- Breakeven per decision: CCoE cost / decisions per year

## CCoE Effectiveness Audit
1. [ ] Survey: Do teams feel CCoE enables or blocks?
2. [ ] Metrics: Deployment velocity by CCoE tenure
3. [ ] Cost: Annual CCoE cost vs avoided cloud sprawl costs
4. [ ] Staffing: Could functions move to product teams?
