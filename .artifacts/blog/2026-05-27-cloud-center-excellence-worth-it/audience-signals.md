# Audience Signals: CCoE Still Worth It in 2026?
## Audience Problem
CCoEs were essential in 2020-2022 (cloud was new). By 2026, are they still necessary? Some organizations maintain large CCoEs that feel like overhead. Need guidance on evaluating whether to downsize, evolve, or eliminate.
## Audience Alignment
- CTO offices
- Cloud leaders reevaluating strategies
- Organizations in CCoE maturity phase
