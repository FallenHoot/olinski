# Contrarian Review

## Strongest critique

The post could be interpreted as formal methodology without presenting measured results.

## Counter-arguments to include

- This is an operating model based on practical workflow design, not a controlled study.
- The model is intentionally lightweight and adaptable by team context.

## Credibility risks

- Overstating impact of AI assistance without hard metrics.
- Sounding like process evangelism instead of practitioner guidance.

## Improvements applied

- Kept claims qualitative and practice-based.
- Added explicit caution that AI is not an authority source.
- Anchored recommendations in practical weekly actions.
