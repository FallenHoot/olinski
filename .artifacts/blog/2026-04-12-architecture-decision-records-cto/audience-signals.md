# Audience Signals: Architecture Decision Records for CTOs
## Audience Problem
CTOs managing large engineering teams struggle with architectural consistency. Decisions are made ad-hoc, not documented, re-made by different teams. ADRs are a lightweight pattern for capturing "why" behind architectural choices. Most CTOs don't have an ADR process.
## Audience Alignment
- CTOs and engineering leaders
- Architecture teams
- Platform teams needing decision governance
