# Editorial Policy

## Core principles

- Be useful before being impressive.
- Prefer practical patterns over abstract theory.
- Use measurable examples where possible.
- Mark uncertainty and assumptions explicitly.

## Approval gate

Every post requires manual approval before publish for:

- Factual correctness
- Confidentiality and customer sensitivity
- Tone and positioning alignment

## Prohibited content

- Confidential customer details
- Internal-only roadmap information
- Sensitive incident details that can identify parties
