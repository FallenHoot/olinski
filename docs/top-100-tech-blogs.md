- Prefer editorial readability over visual gimmicks.
- Favor templates with clear typography, strong post detail pages, and simple navigation.
- Reject themes with noisy animation, weak contrast, or generic card-only layouts.

## Naming patterns to notice

- Personal authority: "Martin Fowler", "Dan Luu", "Simon Willison’s Weblog"
- Functional + audience: "TechBlog", "Engineering", "Developer Blog"
- Metaphor + domain: "Control Plane", "Runbook", "Queue", "Radar"
- Brand-first clarity: "Azure Blog", "GitHub Blog", "Docker Blog"
- Memorable two-word punch: "High Scalability", "Pragmatic Engineer"

## How to use this for naming

1. Pick 10 names from this list you immediately remember after one read.
2. Identify their structure (personal, metaphor, functional, brand-first).
3. Choose one structure and generate 20 candidate names in that style.
4. Test your top 5 with your target audience and LinkedIn profile framing.
