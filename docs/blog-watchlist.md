# Blog Watchlist

Purpose: Maintain a focused list of high-signal blogs and publications that influence topic selection, framing, and quality for zachsBlog.

Supplemental reference list: `docs/top-100-tech-blogs.md`

## How to use this list

1. Review this list monthly.
2. Capture 1-2 notable posts per source in `data/backlog/source-insights-YYYY-MM.md`.
3. Open or update a blog issue when a source reveals:
- a strong argument you want to challenge or extend
- a new framework worth adapting
- a recurring enterprise concern your audience is asking about

## Scoring model

Rate each source from 1-5 in:
- Relevance to pillars
- Evidence quality
- Originality
- Practicality
- Leadership usefulness

Optional weighted score:

`(Relevance x 0.30) + (Evidence x 0.25) + (Originality x 0.20) + (Practicality x 0.15) + (Leadership x 0.10)`

## Pillars

- AI readiness in regulated enterprise
- Reliability and resilience
- Cloud economics and FinOps
- Governance and operating models
- CTO-level leadership execution

## Monthly cadence (recommended)

- Week 1: Scan top 5 sources and capture ideas.
- Week 2: Convert 2 ideas to issue candidates.
- Week 3: Validate novelty against your backlog.
- Week 4: Prioritize one post for next month.
